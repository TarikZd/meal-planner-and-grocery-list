## Product Hunt Description
**Tagline**: Plan meals, auto-build your grocery list, and eat smarter — all in Notion.

**Description**:
Meal planning chaos ends here. The Meal Planner & Grocery List connects your weekly menu to your shopping list via Notion Relations — so when you plan a meal, you can tag every ingredient it needs, and your grocery list builds itself.

✨ **Features**:
- **Weekly Calendar**: A beautiful calendar view shows your meal plan for the entire week.
- **Linked Grocery List**: Every grocery item is tagged to the meal it belongs to — no more mystery shopping trips.
- **Grouped by Aisle**: Filter your grocery list by Category (Produce, Protein, Dairy) to shop faster.
- **Calorie Tracker**: Log estimated calories per meal and track your weekly nutrition.
- **Plug & Play**: Duplicate and plan your first week in under 60 seconds.

Eat well. Shop smart.
